## Joint with tAtAryas
Eechambadi tAtAryas have rights in Thiruvaheendrapuram, Nachiyarkoil.  

Had joint rights with Ahobila Matha in Thiruvallur. They got this rights as a representative of tatacharyas - thus historically was TT rights. ahobilamatha tookover the full admin in 1842. Now maTha's agent, Madabushi family and Nemili family are the hereditary admins.


## Other temples
Other than these four Divyadeshas are under Ahobila Matha nirvaha (Ahobilam Thiruvallur Adhanoor and Pullaboothankudi). 

Even in Srirangam temple the miniscule vadakalai rights and subshrines are either with Ahobila Matha or the Tatacharyas

One can thus conclude all Vadakalai rights historically in Divyadeshas are of Ahobilamatha and Tatacharya families.
