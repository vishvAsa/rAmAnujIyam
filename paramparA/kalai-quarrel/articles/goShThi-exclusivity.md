## tirucherai
>  If he really wants to talk about change in sampradaya in temples, i hv High court documents showing that as late as 1930, in thirucherai ( a divyadesam near Kumbakonam), patrachar (descendant of satakratu tatacharya) had sole rights to veda parayanam, mantra pushpam, thodakkam and adhyapakam in that kshetram. And that ramanuja dayapatram alone was recited back then. But he was the sole local vdk which was otherwise a tk dominant DD. Today the situation is such that a vdk cannot even take part in ghoshti (let alone recite Ramanuja Daya). - RL
