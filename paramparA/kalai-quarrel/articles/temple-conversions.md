## karNATaka
As per early 1800 records all temples in karnataka had vadakalai thiruman - this was due to a protest by parakala mutt people to the diwan that there was an attempt to add thenkalai thirumans in stambhas inside a mysore kingdom temple. So purnaiah put a proclamation about the same.
