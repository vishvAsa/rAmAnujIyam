## नथमुनिप्रभृतिभ्यः प्राक्
Look for the terms like त्रय्यन्त, वेदान्त and such in pAncharAtrAgama-s, the divya-prabandha-s, viShNu purANa and so on. And look for elaborate sections on pAncharAtra in nArAyaNIyam of mahAbhArata. Then observe the defence of pAncharAtra in tarka-pAda of brahma-sUtra-s as correctly interpreted by the bhAgavata-s.

These are the vaiShNava-s and shrI-vaiShNava-s who claimed to be vedAntins. (BTW, even the crypto-buddhu shankara was infact a confused vaiShNava as well.)
