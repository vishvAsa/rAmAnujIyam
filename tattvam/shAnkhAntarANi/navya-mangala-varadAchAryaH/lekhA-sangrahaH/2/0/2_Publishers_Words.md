Vedashastra Poshini Sabha, a century old organization, was established in Mysuru during 1921. The aim of the Sabha is preservation of the Vedas and Shastras in the modern world. So far it has continued to fulfill and achieve its stated objectives to the satisfaction of people who support this cause.

The Sabha has already brought out many publications in this regard. Now this institution is publishing two books related to Pandita Ratnam K.S. Varadacharya, a great innovative scholar of Mysuru.

Panditaratnam K.S. Varadacharya was a Pandit who devoted his life to the study, teaching, interpretation and dissemination of Shastras. As part of his centenary in 2022, a grand function was arranged in Mysuru in the gracious presence of His Holiness Srimad Gangadharendra Saraswati Swamiji of Swarnavalli Samsthanam, Sirsi. The book 'SAMANVAYAVARADA' containing many articles on Shastras written by the scholars was presented to the world on that occasion.

Now, Panditaratnam Varadacharya's precious work 'SATYANVESHANAM', which is a collection of articles written in the divine language Sanskrit and the local language Kannada, is being offered to the world as per the wish of His Holiness.

Vedashastra Poshini Sabha is grateful to Panditaratnam K.S. Varadacharya Centenary Celebration Committee for its great support to this noble work. Sabha is pleased to publish these two works, and grateful to the editorial committee who have rendered a yeoman service for the cause of Sanskrit and Philosophy.

Heartfelt thanks to Mrs. Meera, owner of Annapurna Offset Printers, Mysuru, for her effort in printing the book.

The Sabha expresses gratitude and places on record the contributions made by all those who have worked tirelessly to bring out this publication.

Humble Pranams to HH Srimad Gangadharendra Saraswati Maha Swamiji for the divine blessings and guidance.
