vAdikesari azhagiyamanavALa jeeyar - shishya of periyavAcchAn piLLai - called devarAjamuni. 

> As per tk guruparamparaa he was a shishya of periyavacchan pillai as well as nayanarachanpillai (the former's adopted son). Nayanaracchan Pillai quotes the views of Desika as purvapaksha - SV

देशिक-कृतं न्यासतिलकम् अप्य् उदाहरति। 

> In fact there is some evidence that few of his interpretations are against the tks of his time( which is why some disown him now). He openly supported angatvam of anukUlyasya saGkalpa etc as against others. Didnt call lakshmi as upalaXaNa(though denied her brahmatva, upAyatva- told visheshaNa is as puruShakAra etc). Then was careful not to dismiss varNadharmas directly. - shrIvAsaH
