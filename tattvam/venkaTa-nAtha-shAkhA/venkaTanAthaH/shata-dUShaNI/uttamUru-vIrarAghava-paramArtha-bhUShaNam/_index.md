<details><summary>विस्तारः (द्रष्टुं नोद्यम्)</summary>

Uttamur Swamy is said to have privately remarked that he only used one angle of attack in his paramArtha-bhUShaNam to refute shata-bhUShaNam. If he had used all the angles (nyAya, vyAkaraNa, etc.) the book would have become much much bigger.

इति विजयः। 
</details>
