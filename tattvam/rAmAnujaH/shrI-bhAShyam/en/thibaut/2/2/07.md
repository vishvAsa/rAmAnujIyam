7. And if another inference be made (the result remains unchanged), on account of (the Pradhﾄ］a) being destitute of the power of a knowing subject.

Even if the Pradhﾄ］a were inferred by some reasoning different from the arguments so far refuted by us, our objections would remain in force because, anyhow, the Pradhﾄ］a is devoid of the power of a cognising subject. The Pradhﾄ］a thus cannot be established by any mode of inference.
