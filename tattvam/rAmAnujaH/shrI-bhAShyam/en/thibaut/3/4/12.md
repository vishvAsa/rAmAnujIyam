In the same way as the performance of such works as the Jyotishṭoma sacrifice is something different from the cognition of the true nature of those works; so that vidyā, which effects the highest purpose of man, i. e. devout meditation (dhyāna, upāsanā), is something different from the mere cognition of the true nature of Brahman. Knowledge of that kind has not the most remote connexion even with works.
