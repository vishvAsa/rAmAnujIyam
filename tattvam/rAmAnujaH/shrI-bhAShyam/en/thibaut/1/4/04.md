4. And on account of there being no statement of its being an object of knowledge.

If the text meant the Non-evolved as understood by the Sﾄ］khyas it would refer to it as something to be known; for the Sﾄ］khyas, who hold the theory of Release resulting from the discriminative knowledge of the Evolved, the

 Non-evolved, and the soul, admit that all these are objects of knowledge. Now our text does not refer to the Un-evolved as an object of knowledge, and it cannot therefore be the Pradhﾄ］a assumed by the Sﾄ］khyas.
