<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

> पश्यन्तः पौरुषेयत्वे  
दूषणान्य् उक्तया दिशा ।  
अन्-अन्य-गतयः केचित्  
तन्त्रं नित्यम् **अतिष्ठिपन्** ॥  
</details>

<details><summary>Buitenen</summary>

50\. There are some who notice the logical defects inherent in the view that Scripture of a personal origin,  
which we have explained above, and having no other course open  
posit that Tantra too must be eternal. 
</details>


<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

पश्यन्तः पौरुषेयत्वे दूषणान्युक्तया दिशा ।  
अनन्यगतयः केचित् तन्त्रं नित्यमतिष्ठिपन् ॥  
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

तद् एतत् स्व-हृदय-निहित-विशदतर-  
कतृ-स्मरण-प्रतिहतम् **अभिधीयत**  
इत्य् **उपेक्षणीयम्** ।
</details>

<details><summary>Buitenen</summary>

Against this position, we state that  
it is sublated by the fact that its author,  
who is patent enough and tacitly remembered, is not forgotten at all;  
and hence this position does not deserve our 
notice. 
</details>

</details>
