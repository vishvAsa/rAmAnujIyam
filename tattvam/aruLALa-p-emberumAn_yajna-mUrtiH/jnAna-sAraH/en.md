Vidwan V.K. Srinivasachariar swamy** of sri aruLALaperumAL emperumAnAr thiruvamsam at srIvilliputhUr.

[![vk-srinivasacharyar](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/vk-srinivasacharyar.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/vk-srinivasacharyar.jpg)

kIrthi mUrthy Swami Srinivasachariar is a kumArar (son) of 31st pattam srImadh ubhayavEdhAntha vidwan thirumalai vinjimUr kuppan iyengAr (kuppuswAmy thAthAchAr) swamy.

The thamizh prose by vidwan V.K. Srinivasachariar was published in 2003 panguni-uthrattAdhi, as part of 100th year celebration of Sri U.Ve.kuppuswAmy thAthAchAr.

Text from the original thamizh book, and this translation in English and other languages, are being propagated with permission and blessings of the srI U.Ve. V.K. Srinivasachariar’s kumArar, vidwan srI U.Ve. V.S. Venkatachariar swamy who is decorating 33rd pattam residing at srIvilliputhur thirumALigai, and doing continuous kainkaryams at srinivAsar sannidhi and aruLaLa perumAL EmperumAnAr (aruLALAmAmungaL) sannidhi that are at srI kuppan iyengAr maNdapam in srIvilliputhur.

### About translation to English

When some sishyas approached [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) and requested him to do one more round (uru) of kAlakshEpam of thiruppAvai, he said that only [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) can fully *explain* its meanings, only little gOpikAs whom krishNan loved can better understand its meanings, and only [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) can fully *know* its meanings. So, for the rest of us, we could somewhat explain and somewhat understand the meanings of thiruppAvai, if we tried.

[![Ramanuja_Sriperumbudur](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Ramanuja_Sriperumbudur-235x300.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Ramanuja_Sriperumbudur.jpg)Of course, whatever adiyOngaL are able to write can only be an introduction, because the best source to understand the meanings of thiruppAvai or of any other dhivya prabandham is one’s AchAryan. With AchAryan’s blessings adiyOngaL would like to write here a little about the meanings of thiruppAvai. If you are interested in knowing more, kindly approach your AchAryan.

This effort of translating into English the vyAkyAnams (meanings) from our [pUrvAchArya granthams](http://granthams.koyil.org//purvacharya-literature/) is to spread the awareness of the sweet existence of them, and to popularize them among all srivaishNavas of all ages.

This is not a one-to-one translation of the vyAkyAnam, but tries to bring at least the essence of them.

If you see any important points missing in this effort, kindly let us know so that we could add them as appropriate.

Please continue reading and provide your feedback and comments.

### About the vyAkyAnams for thiruppAvai

**vyAkyAnams for thiruppAvai** have been provided by several [pUrvAchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/).

**3000 padi** – by [periyavAchan piLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/10/05/periyavachan-pillai-english/). (with 3 works by others that provide meanings for difficult words (padha urai))

[![periyavachan-pillai](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/periyavachan-pillai-196x300.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/periyavachan-pillai.jpg)

**6000 padi** – by [azhagiya maNavALa perumAL nAyanAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/15/azhagiya-manavala-perumal-nayanar/)

[![nayanar](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/nayanar-238x300.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/nayanar.jpg)**2000 padi** and **4000 padi** – by [thirunArAyanapuram Ay jananyAchArya](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/04/24/thirunarayanapurathu-ay/)

[![ay-jananyacharyar](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/ay-jananyacharyar-225x300.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/ay-jananyacharyar.jpg)

There are also **svApadEsa vyAkyAnams** (actual inner meanings) available, done by [thirunArAyanapuram Ay jananyAchArya](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/04/24/thirunarayanapurathu-ay/) , [ponnadikkAl jIyar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/30/ponnadikkal-jiyar/) and sudhdha sathvam dhOddachAryar. A list of pramANams (proof/quotes) from vEdham, etc., have also been compiled by our [pUrvAchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/).

**aithihya nirvAgam** for thiruppAvai have been compiled by several swamis. This is about incidents in our [pUrvAchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) time that are related to meanings of thiruppAvai pAsurams *(example: when sishyas approached [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) to do one round (uru) of kAlakshEpam on thiruppAvai, he replied, ‘Even if anyone would be able to do that for thiruppallANdu, it would be hard to find one for providing meanings of thiruppAvai’.)*

**vyAkyAnam for thaniyans** have been provided by [piLLai lOkam jIyar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/04/08/pillai-lokam-jiyar/).

[![pillailokam-jeeyar](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pillailokam-jeeyar-300x203.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pillailokam-jeeyar.jpg)

Without **puthUr swAmi’s** additional efforts to provide meanings for hard-to-understand parts of the vyAkyAnam, one would not be able to understand the original vyAkyAnams at this day and age.

[![Puttur-Swami](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Puttur-Swami-233x300.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Puttur-Swami.jpg)

வ்யாக்யான மூலம் ஸ்ரீமத் மணவாள மாமுநிகளாலும், தமிழில் ஸ்ரீ உ.வே. V.K. ஸ்ரீநிவாஸாசாரியார் அவர்களாலும் எழுதப்பட்டது. இந்த க்ரந்தம் ஸ்ரீ அருளாள பெருமாள் எம்பெருமானார் திருவம்சத்தில் அவதரித்த ஸ்ரீவில்லிபுத்தூர் ஸ்ரீ உ.வே. V.K. ஸ்ரீநிவாஸாசாரியார் ஸ்வாமியால் தமிழில் விரிவுரை இயற்றப்பட்டது.

[![vk-srinivasacharyar](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/vk-srinivasacharyar.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/vk-srinivasacharyar.jpg)

கீர்த்தி மூர்த்தி ஸ்வாமி ஸ்ரீநிவாஸாசாரியார் 31ஆம் பட்டம் ஸ்ரீமத் உபயவேதாந்த வித்வான் திருமலை விஞ்சிமூர் குப்பன் ஐயங்கார் (குப்புஸ்வாமி தாத்தாசார்) ஸ்வாமியின் திருக்குமாரர் ஆவார்.

இந்த தமிழ் வ்யாக்யானம் ஸ்ரீ உ.வே. குப்புஸ்வாமி தாத்தாசாரின் 100ஆவது திருநக்ஷத்திரமான 2003 பங்குனி உத்திரட்டாதி அன்று அச்சிடப்பட்டது.

ஸ்ரீ உ.வே. ஸ்ரீநிவாஸாசாரியாரின் திருக்குமாரரும், இப்பொழுது ஸ்ரீவில்லிபுத்தூர் திருமாளிகையில் 33ஆம் பட்டம் அலங்கரிப்பவரும், ஸ்ரீ குப்பன் அய்யங்கார் மண்டபம் என்று ப்ரசித்தி பெற்ற திருவேங்கடமுடையான் ஸ்ரீ அருளாளப் பெருமாள் எம்பெருமானார் சன்னதியில் தொடர்ந்து பல கைங்கர்யங்களைச் செய்து கொண்டிருப்பவருமான, வித்வான் ஸ்ரீ உ.வே. V.S. வேங்கடாசாரியார் ஸ்வாமி அவர்களின் மங்களாசாசனத்துடன் இத்தை தமிழிலும் மற்ற மொழிகளிலும் வெளியிடுகின்றோம்.
