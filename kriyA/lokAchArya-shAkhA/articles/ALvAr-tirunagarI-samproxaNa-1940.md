Newspaper cutting from 1940-10-10 (Arya-dharmam). 

Azhwar Thirunagari archakar Devarajaiyangar went to Travancore and conducted a utsava in a temple where V5s were permitted entry and came back to ATN to do regular archaka job. 

ATN jiyar didn't take prasadam of Adinathan and Azhwar for Ramanujar Koil as he didn't do prayaschittam and koil no samprokshanam. Athan Thirumaligai stopped their Kainkaryam in Perumal koil to take prasadam for their Aathu perumal and shifted to taking only from north Street Srinivasan temple. People stopped visiting temple, other archakas didn't come for their sannidhi job and temple became empty.

In 28-9-40 all residents of the Agrahara signed a petition to do samprokshana for the temple and submitted to the devasthana executive. The same day afternoon samprokshana works began

Then the next weekend samprokshana was done, archakan forced to do panchagavyadi prayaschittam and things resumed as normal.
