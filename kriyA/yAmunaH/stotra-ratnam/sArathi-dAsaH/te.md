[आळवंदार्](https://guruparamparaitelugu.wordpress.com/2013/09/10/alavandhar/)विशिष्ठाद्वैत सिद्धांतमुन मरियु श्रीवैष्णवसांप्रदायमुन महापंडितुलु मरियु महाङ्ञानि अयिन
[नाथमुनुल](https://guruparamparaitelugu.wordpress.com/2013/09/11/nathamunigal/)मनुमलु. वीरु तम स्तोत्ररत्नमुन द्वयमंत्रमुनु विशदपरचु प्रधानमैन प्राप्यं मरियु प्रापकमुलनु
विवरंगा तॆलियपरिचारु. मन पूर्वाचार्युलु अनुग्रहिंचिन संस्कृत ग्रंथाललो दीनिनि मॊदटिदिगा परिगणिस्तारु.

इळयाळ्वार् (श्रीरामानुजुलु)नु आळवंदार् शिष्युनिगा चेयुटकु पॆरियनंबि कांचीपुरमुनकु वॆळतारु.
आ समयमुन तिरुकच्चिनंबि आङ्ञानुसारं इळयाळ्वार् शालक्किणर् (नूति/बावि)नुंडि देवपॆरुमाळ् तिरुवाराथनकु तीर्थकैंकर्यमु चेयुचुंडिरि. पॆरियनंबि स्तोत्ररत्नमु नुंडि श्लोकमुलनु इळयाळ्वार् वॆळ्ळुदारिन निलबडि पठिस्तारु. ई श्लोकमुनु विनि इळयाळ्वार् अभिनिवेशं पॊंदि सांप्रदायमुलोकि प्रवेशिस्तारु.
ऎंपॆरुमानार् गा प्रसिद्धि प्रसिद्धिचॆंदिन इळयाळ्वार् ई स्तोत्रमुयंदु अत्यंत अभिनिवेशं कलिगि तम श्रीवैकुंठगद्यमुन ई स्तोत्रमुनुंडि चाला गद्यमुलनु उट्टंकिंचारु(स्वीकरिंचारु).

पॆरियवाच्चान् पिळ्ळै ई प्रबंधमुनकु विशदमैन व्याख्यानमुनु कृपचेशारु. ई स्तोत्रमुलोनि निगूढार्थमुलनु वॆलिकितीसि विस्तारमैन व्याख्यानमुनु अनुग्रहिंचारु.

ई ग्रंथमुनकु उन्न तनियन्लनु तॆलुसुकुंदां.

आळवंदार्, काट्टुमन्नार् कोयिल्

ई ग्रंथमुनकु उन्न तनियन्लनु तॆलुसुकुंदां.

*स्वादयन्निह सर्वेषां त्रय्यंतार्थं सुदुर्ग्रहं ।*  
*स्तोत्रयामास योगींद्रः तं वन्दे यामुनाह्वयं॥*

ग्राह्यमुनकु दुर्गमैन वेदांत रहस्यमुलनु सुलभमुगा ग्राह्यमगुनट्लु तम स्तोत्ररत्नमुन विशदीकरिंचिन, योगुललो श्रेष्ठुलैन यामुनाचार्युलकु वंदनं.

*यत् पदां भोरुह ध्यान विध्वस्ताशेषकल्मषः ।*  
*वस्तुतामुपयातो2हं यामुनेयं नमामि तं॥*

ऎवरि दिव्यकृपतो ना कल्मषमुलन्नी नाशनमु चेंदिनवो मरियु ऒक वस्तुवुगा गुर्तिंचबड्डानो पूर्वमु असत् (अचेतनमु)गा उंडि यामुनाचार्युल पादमुल ध्यानमुतो प्रस्तुतं सत्(आत्म/ चेतनमु) गा भाविस्तुन्नानो आश्री यामुनाचार्युलनकु नमस्करिस्तुन्नानु.

## स्तोत्ररत्नं – अवतारिक
श्री- श्रीमते शठकोपाय नमः श्रीमते रामानुजाय नमः श्रीमद्वरवरमुनये नमः

[स्तोत्ररत्नं](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/index.php/2017/01/sthothra-rathnam-telugu/)

####### [![nathamuni-alavandhar](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/nathamuni-alavandhar.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/nathamuni-alavandhar.jpg)

####### नथमुनुलु, आळवंदार्लु – काट्टु मन्नार् कोयिल्

तिरुवाय्मॊळि 1.1.1 व पाशुरमुलो “मयर्वऱ मदि नलं अरुळिनन्” (भगवानुडि निर्मलमैन ज्ञान भक्तुलकु अनुग्रहपात्रुलैन नम्माळ्वार्लु) अनि वर्णिंचिनट्लु सर्वेश्वरुडैन परमात्म तम निर्हेतुक कृपतो तन रूप गुण स्वरूपालनु, ऐश्वर्यादुलनु वॆल्लडि चेशारु. आ ज्ञानमे भक्ति रूप आपन्न ज्ञानमु (ज्ञानमु परिपक्वमै भक्तिगा मारुट) गा मारि, परभक्तितो (भक्तिलोनि प्रथम दश – पूर्ण भगवत् ज्ञानं) भगवत् गुणालनु नम्माळ्वार्लु आस्वादिंचारु. आ तरुवात परज्ञान (भक्तिलोनि तरुवात दश – भगवानुडिनि प्रत्यक्षंगा चूसिनट्लुगा भाविंचडं) दशकु वारु चेरुकॊनि, दानि फलितंगा परम भक्तिनि (भक्तिलोनि आखरि दश – परमपदान्नि अधिरोहिंचे मुंदु दश) पॊंदारु. तत्फलितंगा, चांधोग्य उपनिषत् 8.12.2लो “परं ज्योतिर् उपसंपध्य” (परम कांति अयिन नारायणनु पॊंदुट), मुंडकोपनिषत् 3.1.3 “परमं साम्यं उपैति” (अष्ट गुणाल द्वारा जीवात्म भगवानुडि साम्यं पॊंदुताडु), पॆरिय तिरुमॊळि 11.8.5 व पाशुरं “तम्मैये ऒक्क अरुळ् शॆय्वर्” (आ आत्म तनला मारेला भगवानुडु अनुग्रहिस्ताडु), तिरुवाय्मॊळि 3.3.1 “वळुविला अडिमै शॆय्य वेंडुं” (निर्विराम सेव चेयालने कोरिक), तिरुवाय्मॊळि 10.10.11 “अवावऱ्ऱु वीडु पॆऱ्ऱ” (नम्माळ्वार्, तन कोरिकलन्निटिनी नॆरवेर्चुकोवडानिकि मुक्तिनि पॊंदाडु) लो चॆप्पिनट्लु आळ्वार् तन निज स्वरूपान्नि ग्रहिंचि, भगवत् कैंकर्यान्नि पॊंदारु.

द्राविड उपनिषत् (तिरुवाय्मॊळि) यॊक्क ई रहस्य ज्ञानान्नि नम्माळ्वार्ल अनुग्रहंतो पॆरिय मुदलियार् (नाथमुनुलु) पॊंदारु. आळवंदार्लु वारि आचार्युलैन नाथमुनुल द्वारा ई रहस्य ज्ञानान्नि पॊंदि, वाटिपै चिंतन चेस्तू, संसारुल स्थितिनि चूसि भरिंचलेक, तम भाधनु आपुकोलेक, ई रहस्य ज्ञानान्नि अज्ञानुलै आ अंदरिकी तॆलियजेयालनि भाविंचि, उपनिषत् सारमु, अत्यंत कठिनमु अयिन ई गोप्य ज्ञानान्नि सरळं चेसि स्तोत्र रूपंलो मनमुंदु उंचारु.

भगवत् विषयं कानि संदर्भाललो स्तोत्रं अंटे मुखस्तुति (लेनि गुणालु उन्नट्टुगा चॆप्पि प्रशंसिंचडं) अनि अर्थं. श्री स्तवं 3व श्लोकंलो “स्तोत्रं नाम किमामनंति” (कवुलु पॊगिडिनट्लु), श्री रंग राज स्थवं पूर्व शतकंलो “अन्यत्रातद्गुणोक्तिः” (भगवत् विषयालु मिनहा, स्तुतिंचडं अंटे लेनि गुणाल गुरिंचि माट्लाडटं/पॊगडटं अनि अर्थं) अनि चॆप्पबडि उंदि. कानी, भगवत् विषयं वर्णनातीतमैनदनि तैत्रीय उपनिषत्तु आनंदवल्लि 9.1 व श्लोकंलो “यतो वाचो निवर्तंते” (भगवत् गुणालनु वर्णिंचलेक ऊहकंदनि वाडनि शब्दालु/पदालु दिक्कुतॆलियक अलसिपोयि तिरिगि वच्चायि) अनि चॆप्पिनट्लु, केन उपनिषत्तु 2.3 लो “अविज्ञातं विजानतां” (अन्नीऎरिगिनवारिके ब्रह्मंनु पूर्तिगा ऎरुगलेमु अनि तॆलुसु) मॊदलैन प्रमाणालु घोषिस्तुन्नायि. अटुवंटि भगवंतुडिनि इक्कड ऎला स्तुतिंचगलरु? आयननु पूर्तिगा ग्रहिंचलेमु कानी मनं आयननु स्तुतिंचगलमु. ऎंदुकंटे

- श्रीविष्णु सहस्रनामं “स्थव्यः स्थवप्रियः” (आतडु स्रुतिंचदगिनवाडु, स्तुति प्रियुडु) अनि
  चॆप्पबडिंदि. अनगा भगवानुडु स्तुति प्रियुडु अनि अर्थं.
- तिरुवाय्मॊळि 2.2.11 व पाशुरं “एत्त एळुलगुं कॊंडा” (मुल्लोकालनु कॊलिचि अंदरिचे
  स्तुतिंपबड्डाडु), तिरुवाय्मॊळि 4.3.10 व पाशुरंलो “ऎत्त ऎत्त ऎंगेय्‌ दुं” (अंदरू
  स्तुंतिचिना लॆक्कलेनन्नि मंगळगुणालुन्नवाडु आतडु अनि) अनि चॆप्पबडिंदि. अनगा लॆक्कलेनन्नि गुणालु
  उन्नवाडु आतडु अनि अर्थं.
- भगवत् स्तुति तप्प मन नोटि नुंडि मरॊकरि प्रशंस राकूडदु.

मरॊक विवरण \[प्राप्यं (लक्ष्यं), प्रापकं (माध्यमं) पै दृष्टि सायिंचे परिचयं\].

\[मॊदट, लक्ष्यं विवरिंचबडिंदि.\] पुरुषार्थालु नालुगनि श्रीविष्णु पुराणं 1.18.22 लो “धर्मार्थ काम मोक्षाक्याः पुरुषार्था उदाहृताः । इदं चतुष्टयं” अनि विवरिंचबडि उंदि. अनगा आ नालुगु – धर्मं (धर्म मार्गं, अर्थ (संपद), काम (विषय वांछलु), मोक्षालु मन लक्ष्यालनि अर्थं). पॆरिय तिरुमडल् 11व पाशुरंलो “मन्नुं अऱं पॊरुळ् इन्बं वीडु” (धर्मं, अर्थ, काम, मोक्षं). वीटिलो, धर्मं अनेदि मन लक्ष्यं, पैगा साधनं कूडा \[अनगा, धर्मबद्धमैन पनुलु चेयडं मन साधनं, अलागे मन लक्ष्यं कूडा\],कानी इदि कष्टंतो कूडुकुन्नदि. अर्थं अनेदि ऒक स्वतंत्र लक्ष्यं, अयिनप्पटिकी, इदि तात्कालिकमैनदि, निलुपुकोवडं कष्टं, इतर लक्ष्यालनु साधिंचेंदुकु अदि साधनं वंटिदि. कामं कूडा ऒक स्वतंत्र लक्ष्यमे, अयिनप्पटिकी तात्कालिकमैनदि, निराधारमैनदि, विनाशकरमैनदि \[ज्ञानं वल्ल एर्पडिन स्वरूपानिकि विरुद्धंगा\], असह्यकरमैनदि. पैगा कामं अनेदि मोक्षानिकि अड्डंकि अनि मोक्ष शास्त्रंलो मन पॆद्दलु विवरिंचारु. आळवंदार्लु वीटन्निंटिनी पक्कन पॆट्टि मोक्षंपैने तम दृष्ठि उंचारु. मोक्षंलो कूडा, कैवल्य मोक्षमु काकुंडा कैंकर्य मोक्षमुपैन तम दृष्टि उंचारु. ऎंदुकंटे, ऎंतो प्रयास पडि साधिंचबडिनप्पटिकी, भगवत् अनुभवंतो पोल्चिते अदि अल्पमैन फलमनि, तम दृष्टि ए कल्मशंलेनि भगवत् प्राप्तिपैन केंद्रीकरिंचारु. अंते कादु, श्रीविष्णु पुराणं 6.5.58, 6.5.59 लो “निरस्थाति शयाह्लाद सुखभावैकलक्षणा” (परमानंदं तम परिचयंगा उन्नवाडु) लो चॆप्पिनट्लु भगवानुडु पूर्णआनंदमयुडु अनि विवरिंचबडिंदि. तैत्रीय उपनिषत् आनंदवल्लि 7 लो “एष ह्येवानंदयाति” (आतडु जीवात्मकु केवलं आनंदान्नि मात्रमे प्रसादिस्ताडु) अनि चॆप्पिनट्लु, मुंडक उपनिषत् लो “परमं साम्यमुपैति” (परमात्मनु पॊंदडं), पॆरिय तिरुमॊळि 11.3.5 व पाशुरंलो “तम्मैये ऒक्क अरुळ् शॆय्वर्” (तननु आराधिंचे वारिनि तन वले अय्येला अनुग्रहिस्तारु) अनि चॆप्पिनट्लु आतडु तम भक्तुलपै कूडा अदे परमानंदान्नि कुर्पिस्ताडु अनि अर्थं. श्री भगवत् गीत 8.14 व श्लोकंलो “तस्याहं सुलभः” (नन्नु पॊंदडं सुलभं) अनि चॆप्पिनट्लु अतनिनि सुलभंगा पॊंदगलमु. कनुक भगवत् प्राप्ति उत्तम लक्ष्यंगा परिगणिंचारु मन पॆद्दलु.

- तैत्तीय उपनिषत्तु नारायणवल्लि 50 “न्यासमाहुर् मनीषिणो ब्रह्माणं” (परमात्मनि न्यासगा पंडितुलु
  विवरिस्तारु)
- तैत्तीय उपनिषत्तु नारायणवल्लि 49 “न्यास इति ब्रह्म” (ब्रह्ममे न्यासं)
- तैत्तीय उपनिषत्तु नारायणवल्लि 50 “तस्मान् न्यासमेषां तपसां अतिरिक्तमाहुः” (अंदुवलन, ई न्यास
  (शरणागति) अत्युन्नतमैन तपस्सुगा चॆप्पबडिंदि).
- यजुर् कांडम् 6.5 “तस्मादपि वद्यं प्रपन्नं न प्रतिप्रयच्छंति” (शरणागति चेसिनट्लयिते, अतनु
  चंपबडटानिकि अर्हत उन्नप्पटिकी, इतरुलतो पोल्चकूडदु).
- श्री रामायणं अयोध्य कांडं 31.2 “स भ्रातुस् शरणौ गाडं निपीड्य रघुनंदन” (लक्ष्मणुडु तन
  सोदरुडैन श्रीरामुनि पादालनु गट्टिगा पट्टुकुनि माट्लाडाडु)
- श्री रामायणं युद्ध कांडं 18.33 “सकृदेव प्रपन्नाय” (ऒकसारि शरणागति चेसिना अतनिनि ऎप्पटिकी
  वदुलनु).
- श्रीविष्णु पुराणं 5.23.47 “सोहं त्वं शरमपारं अप्रमेयं” (तन कंटे गॊप्पवारु ऎवरू लेनि,
  अंतुलेनि, ऊहकि चिक्कनि वाडिकि नेनु शरणागतुडैनानु)
- श्री भागवतं 11.5.41लो “सर्वात्मना यश् शरणं शरण्यं नारायणं लोक गुरुं प्रपन्नः” (ऎवरैते
  श्रीमन्नारायणुनिकि पूर्ण शरणागति चेयुनो, अतडु देवतलु, ऋषुलु, बंधुवुलु, पितृलकु ऋणपडि
  उंडडु. वारिकि दसुडु काडु.
- श्री भगवत्गीत 7.14 “मामेवये प्रपद्यंते मायां एतां तरंति ते” (नाकु शरणागति चेसिन वाडु अज्ञान
  सागरान्नि सुलभंगा दाटिपोताडु).
- श्री भगवत्गीत 18.62 “तमेव शरणं गच्छ सर्वभावेन भारत” (ओ भरत उत्तराधिकारि! आ भगवानुनिके
  शरणागति चेयुडु)
- श्री भगवत्गीत 18.66 “सर्वधर्मान् परित्यज्य” (अन्नि धर्मालनु विडिचिपॆट्टि नाकु मात्रमे शरणागति
  चेयुडु)
- श्रीविष्णु पुराणं 1.9.73 “तावतार्थि स्तता वांचा” (नीकु शरणागति चेसेवरकु मात्रमे, अतनिकि संपद
  पैन कोरिक उंटुंदि, संपादिंचलेदने चिंतन उंटुंदि)

भगवानुनि स्तुतिंचडानिकि बयलुदेरिन आळवंदार्लु ऎंदुकु तम आचार्युनि \[मॊदटि मूडु श्लोकाल्लो नाथमुनिनि स्तुतिंचारु\] स्तुतिंचडं प्रारंभिंचारु? “कर्णे स्पृष्टे कडिम् चालयति” (वारि चॆवुलु ताकिनप्पुडु, तन तुंटिनि कदिलिंचॆनु) अने सामॆत लागा उंदि कादा? वास्तवानिकि, इदि क्रिंदि कारणालकु संबंधिंचिनदि-

- भगवत् स्तुति चेसेंदुकु तननु (आळवंदार्) सृष्टिंचिनंदुकु कृतज्ञत
- भगवानुनि स्तुतिंचगलिगे ज्ञानं कलिगि उंडालि
- प्रबंधान्नि अड्डंकुलु लेकुंडा पूर्ति चेयमनि प्रार्थिंचडं

ई स्तुति मनसुलो चेयगलमा? ई प्रबंधं नेर्चुकोवालनुकुने वारिकि गॊप्प उपकारं अवुतुंदि.

कृतज्ञततो आचार्युनि सेविंचालनि अनेक प्रमाणालु घोषिस्तुन्नायि.

- तैत्तिरीय उपनिषत्तु शीक्षावल्लि 11 “आचार्य देवो भव” (आचार्युनि देवुडिगा भाविंचुट)
- यजुर् आरण्यकं 4 “नम ऋषिभ्यो मंत्रकृध्भ्यः” (मंत्रान्नि जीर्णिंचुकुन्न ऋषुलनु नेनु
  आराधिस्तानु)
- आपस्तंब सूत्रं 1.1.6 “स हि विध्यातस् तं जनयति । तच्च्रेष्टं जन्म ॥” (आचार्युडु
  शिष्युनिकि ज्ञानोदयं चेसि पुनर्जन्मनु इस्ताडु; अदि देह जन्म कंटे गॊप्पदि)
- विष्णु तत्वं “मंत्रे तद्देवतायांच तता मंत्र प्रदे गुरौ” (मंत्रानिकि, आ मंत्रंलो उन्न देवतकु,
  आ मंत्रान्नि उपदेशिंचिन आचार्युनिकि कट्टुबडि उंडालि)

विलुवैन रत्नान्नि मनकॆवरैना इस्ते, इच्चिन व्यक्ति पट्ल कृतज्ञततो उंडटं मर्याद. दीनिकि शास्त्र प्रमाणं अवसरं लेदु कदा?

मनलो ज्ञानोदयं कलिगिंचिन आचार्युनि स्तुतिंचुट मन पॆद्दलु समान्यंगा आचरिंचारु.

- मीमासक – मीमासक कारिका “अभिवंध्य गुरुनादौ शिष्य धीपद्मिनीरवीन्” (शिष्युलुकु ज्ञानं, कमलानिकि
  सूर्युनि वंटि आचार्युलनु मॊदट सेविंचडं)
- मनु – मनु स्मृति 2.218 “यथा कनन् कनित्रेण” (मनुष्युडु गॊय्यि तव्वि नीळ्ळु तॆच्चिनट्लु,
  शिष्युडु गुरुवुनु सेविंचडं द्वारा ज्ञानान्नि पॊंदुताडु)
- वैशंपायन ऋषि – महाभारतं आदि पर्वं 1.27 “यस्य प्रसादात् वक्ष्यामि नारायण कथं इमाम्” (ज्ञान
  तेजस्सु कलिगिन व्यास भगवानुनि अनुग्रहंतो अपरिमितुडैन नारायणुनि चरित्रनु पठिंगलुगुतुन्नानु.
  आतनिकि ना वंदनालु).
- मनु – मनु स्मृति 2.117 “लौकिकं वैधिकं वा’पि” (प्रापंचिक ज्ञानान्नि, शास्त्रोक्तमैन ज्ञानान्नि,
  ब्रह्म ज्ञानान्नि नेर्पिंचिन गुरुवुनि मुंदु सेविंचालि)

इला उंडगा, आळवंदार्लु तम स्वाचार्युलु (मणक्काल् नंबि) नि सेविंचुटकु बदुलु तम आचार्युल गुरुवुनि ऎंदुकु सेविंचारु? तम स्वाचार्युलनु स्तुतिंचडं कंटे आचार्युडु कोरुकुन्न दानिनि स्तुतिंचडं अति उत्तमं; आळवंदार्लु पॆरिय मुदलियार् (नाथमुनुलु) स्तुतिंचुट, तम आचार्युनिकि संतृप्ति कलिगिंचुननि आळवंदार्लु इला चेसारु. नाथमुनुलनु आळवंदार्लु आश्रयिंचुटकु रॆंडु कारणालुन्नायि-

- नाथमुनुलु, नम्माळ्वार्ल कृपतो, आळ्वार् कि भगवान् पट्ल उन्न अपारमैन भक्ति कारणंगा आर्जिंचिन
  ज्ञानान्नि पॊंदि, आ ज्ञानान्नि आयन वरके परितं काकुंडा दानिनि भावि तरानिकु पंचालनि परितपिंचिचारु, मन
  संप्रदायानिकि दर्शन स्थापकुडु (अध्यक्ष आचार्युडु) अयिनारु.
- मणक्काळ् नंबि अळवंदार्लतो “ई सूत्रालन्नी नाथमुनिचे विवरिंचबड्डायि; अतनि दिव्य तिरुवडि संबंधमे
  नीकु अत्युन्नत लक्ष्यानिकि साधनं.” अन्नारु.

ई कारणंगाने, श्रीविष्णु सहस्रनाम भाष्यंलो भट्टर् “प्रथममपि गुरुं नाथमीडे” (प्रथमाचार्युलैन नाथामुनुलनु नेनु सेविस्तुन्नानु) अनि अन्नारु.

वेदं प्रारंभंलो मरियु मुगिंपुलो वेदानिकि मूलमैन ‘प्रणवं’ पठिंचिनट्लुगा, इक्कड, आचार्युनि प्रारंभंलो मरियु मुगिंपुलो स्तुतिस्तारु, मध्यलो भगवानुनि स्तुतिस्तारु. मॊदटि मूडु श्लोकाल्लो आळवंदार्लु नाथमुनुलनु सेविस्तुन्नारु.

मरुसटि शीर्षिकलो मॊदटि श्लोकमु चूद्दामु,


## sthOthra rathnam
SrI: SrImathE SatakOpAya nama: SrImathE rAmAnujAya nama: SrImath varavaramunayE nama:

[![vishnu-lakshmi](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/vishnu-lakshmi.jpg)j![alavandhar-nathamunigal](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/ALavandhAr-nAthamunigaL.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/ALavandhAr-nAthamunigaL.jpg)[ALavandhAr](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/alavandhar/) and [nAthamunigaL](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/nathamunigal/) – [kAttu mannAr kOyil](http://kattumannarkoyil.koyil.org)

e-book – <https://1drv.ms/b/s!AiNzc-LF3uwygwb9qN-HcojKb1iH>

[ALavandhAr](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/alavandhar/), a great scholar of viSishtAdhvaitha sidhdhAntham and SrIvaishNava sampradhAyam and the grand son of the revered AchArya, [nAthamunigaL](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/nathamunigal/), revealed the most essential principles of prApyam (goal) and prApakam (means) through detailed elaboration of dhvaya mahA manthram in his sthOthra rathnam. This is the earliest known samskritha sthOthra grantham of our [pUrvAchAryas](http://acharyas,koyil.org) which is available for us now.

[periya nambi](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/periya-nambi/) went to kAnchIpuram with the desire of facilitating [iLaiyAzhwAr (SrI rAmAnuja)](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/emperumanar/) to become a Sishya of ALavandhAr. iLaiyAzhwAr was bringing thIrtham (water) from sALaik kiNaRu (a well) as kainkaryam for dhEvap perumAL under the guidance of [thirukkachchi nambi](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/thirukkachi-nambi/). periya nambi recites some SlOkams from sthOthra rathnam which attracts iLaiyAzhwAr very much and brings iLaiyAzhwAr into our sampradhAyam. iLaiyAzhwAr who goes on to be famously known as emperumAnAr, had great attachment for this prabandham and used several passages from this prabandham in his [SrIvaikuNta gadhyam](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/index.php/2016/03/srivaikunta-gadhyam/) too.

[periyavAchchAn piLLai](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/periyavachchan-pillai/) has written an elaborate vyAkyAnam (commentary) for this divine prabandham. The esoteric meanings of the sthOthrams are eloquently explained by periyavAchchAn piLLai in his commentary.

We shall now see the thaniyans for this divine treatise.

## Introduction
SrI: SrImathE SatakOpAya nama: SrImathE rAmAnujAya nama: SrImath varavaramunayE nama:

[![paramapadhanathan](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/paramapadhanathan.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/paramapadhanathan.jpg)

[SrIman nArAyaNan](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) with [SrI dhEvi (SrI mahAlakshmi)](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/), bhUdhEvi, nILA dhEvi and nithyasUris in paramapadham

[SrIman nArAyaNan](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) is the supreme God who is filled with unlimited and wonderful auspicious qualities. Out of his divine and unconditional grace, he blessed selected [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s to become [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) (great saints who preached pure and exclusive devotion towards [SrIman nArAyaNan](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/)). Though He was the supremely independent master of the nithyasUris (eternally free souls) and mukthAthmAs (liberated souls), he wanted to rid himself of the one agony he had.

That agony is him suffering for the suffering of the bound souls in the material world. Him being the supreme father of every one, he was unable to bear the suffering of his children who are caught in the cycle of birth and death in the material world. One may question, if the supreme God will have any agony/sorrow – him being sathya kAma (one who can fulfill all his desires) and sathya sankalpa (one who can accomplish anything). Our [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) have explained that him feeling the pain for the suffering souls is also his auspicious quality. Just like an all-capable father who is happily living with his one child will still worry for his other child who is staying away from him on his own and struggling, [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/), though being all-capable, he is feeling the pain looking at the plight of the [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s in samsAram (material world) who are suffering since time-immemorial due to being covered by ignorance and lacking of true knowledge.

To facilitate the upliftment of such suffering [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s, he gives body and senses for the [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s during srushti (creation), reveals the sAsthram (scriptures), himself descends in to the material world as various incarnations like SrI rAma, krishNa, etc. Even after all of these, the [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s are not really willing to learn the true knowledge and accept his supremacy. Just like a hunter will catch a deer using another trained deer, He decides to uplift [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s through other [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s who are already in the material world. So he chooses a select few of them, blesses them with divine knowledge and makes them [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/). AzhwAr means one who is immersed in bhagavath vishayam (spiritual matters). These [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) appeared in various holy places in the southern part of bhAratha dhEsam as foretold by vEdha vyAsa in SrI bhAgavatham. Totally, [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) are said to be 10 in number. [poigai AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/10/22/mudhalazhwargal/) (sarO yOgi),[bhUthaththAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/10/22/mudhalazhwargal/) (bhUtha yOgi), [pEyAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/10/22/mudhalazhwargal/) (mahathAhvaya), [thirumazisai AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/16/thirumazhisai-azhwar/) (bhakthi sAra), [nammAzhwAr (satakOpa/parAnkusa)](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/18/nammazhwar-english/), [kulasEkara AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/18/kulasekara-azhwar/),
[periyAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/20/periyazhwar/) (vishNuchiththa),[thoNdaradippodi AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/08/thondaradippodi-azhwar/) (bhakthAngri rENu),
[thiruppANAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/21/thiruppanazhwar/) (muni vAhana yOgi) and[thirumangai AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/23/thirumangai-azhwar/) (parakAla). They are also said to be 12 in number if [madhurakavi AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/17/madhurakavi-azhwar/) and [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) (gOdhA – an incarnation of bhUdhEvi nAchiyAr, one of the divine consorts of [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/)) are included. While the 10 [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) are said to be exclusively devoted to [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/), [madhurakavi AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/17/madhurakavi-azhwar/) and [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) were totally devoted to their [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/), [nammAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/18/nammazhwar-english/) and [periyAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/20/periyazhwar/) respectively.

[![Azhwars](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Azhwars-1024x235.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Azhwars.jpg)

[AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/)

[AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) sung many pAsurams (verses) in glorification of [SrIman nArAyaNan](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/). These pAsurams total up to 4000 verses approximately and are thus called 4000 dhivya prabandham. dhivya means divine and prabandham means literature (that which captures [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/) himself). They brought out the essence of vEdham/vEdhAntham in simple and chaste thamizh. The whole purpose of these dhivya prabandhams is to uplift the [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s by transmitting true knowledge. Several hundreds of years after the time of [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/), many [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) starting with [nAthamunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/22/nathamunigal/), centered with [SrI rAmAnuja](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) and ending with [mAmunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/23/azhagiya-manavala-mamunigal/) appeared and preached the divine message of [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/). While less intelligent people considered[AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) pAsurams as simple thamizh songs, the most intelligent [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) established that these pAsurams bring out the ultimate philosophy of [SrIman nArAyaNan](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) being the means (to be uplifted from this material world) and the goal (to be situated in ones true nature of eternal service to [SrIman nArAyaNan](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) in paramapadham – spiritual world). Our [pUrvAchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) fully relished the dhivya prabandhams and centered their life around learning them, teaching them and living by those pAsurams.

[![azhwar-madhurakavi-nathamuni](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/azhwar-madhurakavi-nathamuni.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/azhwar-madhurakavi-nathamuni.jpg)[nammAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/18/nammazhwar-english/) with[madhurakavi AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/17/madhurakavi-azhwar/) and [nAthamunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/22/nathamunigal/) in either side

There was a dark period in the history where the dhivya prabandhams were lost in time after the time of [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/). Eventually, it was [nAthamunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/22/nathamunigal/) who took lots of hardships to locate AzhwAr thirunagari which is the birth place of [nammAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/18/nammazhwar-english/) and he learnt all the 4000 dhivya prabandham with their meanings by the divine grace of [nammAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/18/nammazhwar-english/). [nAthamunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/22/nathamunigal/) classified the 4000 dhivya prabandhams into 4 parts as we see today, taught them to his sishyas and brought out the importance of these dhivya prabandhams. He added kaNNinuN chiRu thAmbu into 4000 dhivya prabandhams in honour of[madhurakavi AzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/01/17/madhurakavi-azhwar/) who showed unflinching devotion towards [nammAzhwAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/18/nammazhwar-english/).

[![Ramanuja_Sriperumbudur](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Ramanuja_Sriperumbudur.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Ramanuja_Sriperumbudur.jpg)

[SrI rAmAnuja](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/)

[SrI rAmAnuja (emperumAnAr)](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) who is considered as an incarnation of Adhi sEsha and who appeared a century later received the same through the guru paramparA by studying the same under various [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) per the desire of [yAmunAchArya](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/01/alavandhar/). He then spread the glories of [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) and their works to all sections of the society and greatly facilitated the propagation of this SrIvaishNava sampradhAyam. It is because of his great contribution, this sampradhAyam was declared to be known as [SrI rAmAnuja](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) dharsanam by [SrI ranganAtha](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) himself. Also, rAmAnusa nURRanthAdhi which was sung in glorification of [SrI rAmAnuja](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) by [thiruvarangaththu amudhanAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/03/26/thiruvarangathu-amudhanar/) was added into 4000 dhivya prabandham by the [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) due to his greatness. This rAmAnusa nURRanthAdhi is glorified as “prapanna gAyathri” – that which is to be recited compulsorily by all prapannas (ones who are surrendered) every day, just like how gAyathri manthram is to be recited by brAhmaNas everyday.

[![nampillai-goshti1](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/nampillai-goshti1.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/nampillai-goshti1.jpg)[nampiLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/16/nampillai-english/) kAlakshEpa (lecture) gOshti (assembly)

[nampiLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/16/nampillai-english/) is an important AchArya (coming in the parampArA after [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/), [embAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/07/embar/), [bhattar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/11/parasara-bhattar/) and [nanjIyar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/14/nanjiyar-english/)) who should be mentioned at this juncture. He was living in SrIrangam and was the leader of SrIvaishNava sampradhAyam during his times. It was during his period in SrIrangam, the meanings of 4000 dhivya prabandham were given great importance. He was engaged in giving lectures everyday close to the sanctum sanctorium of [periya perumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) – [SrI ranganAtha](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/). It is explained that even [periya perumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) would stand up and try to peek through the window to hear his lectures. [nampiLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/16/nampillai-english/)‘s sishyas also greatly contributed to propagate the meanings of these dhivya prabandhams. [periyavAchchAn piLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/10/05/periyavachan-pillai-english/) who is a sishya of[nampiLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/16/nampillai-english/) and also known as vyAkyAna chakkaravarthy (emperor among commentators) wrote commentaries for all dhivya prabandhams and his contribution is greatly hailed by our [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/). [vadakku thiruvIdhi piLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/17/vadakku-thiruveedhi-pillai-english/), who is another sishya of [nampiLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/16/nampillai-english/), documented the lectures given by [nampiLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/16/nampillai-english/) on thiruvAimozhi and this document became to be known as eedu 36000 padi (the most cherished vyAkyAnam of thiruvAimozhi).

[![pillailokacharya-goshti](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pillailokacharya-goshti.jpeg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pillailokacharya-goshti.jpeg)piLLai lOkAchAryar kAlakshEpa gOshti

After [nampiLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/16/nampillai-english/)‘s time, [piLLai lOkAchAryar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/18/pillai-lokacharyar/) leads the sath sampradhAyam and documents all the most confidential matters of dhivya prabandham in his rahasya granthams. These confidential matters were explained by various [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) at various times in various granthams.[piLLai lOkAchAryar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/18/pillai-lokacharyar/) consolidated those and documented them in his 18 primary works. But towards the end of his life, tragedy struck SrIrangam in the form of mughal invaders who destroyed everything there.[piLLai lOkAchAryar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/18/pillai-lokacharyar/) arranged for the safe journey of namperumAL (SrI ranganAtha – uthsavar) and escaped from the invaders from there along with namperumAL. Shortly in to the journey, he ascended to paramapadham due to the difficult journey through forests at such advanced age. Then came a lengthy period of suffering for the whole of SrIvaishNava community. After few decades, when peace prevailed and invaders were driven out, namperumAL eventually returned to SrIrangam.

[![srisailesa-thanian-small](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/srisailesa-thanian-small.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/srisailesa-thanian-small.jpg)[mAmunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/23/azhagiya-manavala-mamunigal/) kAlakshEpa gOshti – “srI sailEsa dhayApAthram” thanian submission

Finally, [maNavALa mAmunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/23/azhagiya-manavala-mamunigal/) who is considered as an incarnation of [SrI rAmAnuja](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) himself, appeared in [AzhwAr thirunagari](http://azhwarthirunagari.koyil.org/). He became a sishya of[thiruvAimozhi piLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/19/thiruvaimozhi-pillai/) and learned all the esoteric meanings of vEdham, vEdhAntham and dhivya prabandhams from his own father and [thiruvAimozhi piLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/19/thiruvaimozhi-pillai/). Under the orders of[thiruvAimozhi piLLai](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/19/thiruvaimozhi-pillai/) (his AchArya), he reached SrIrangam and spent the rest of his life in rejuvenating the sath sampradhAyam to its past glories. He personally searched for all the lost literature, studied them, recorded them in palm leaves with great difficulty and preserved them for future generations to come. Fittingly, in appreciating his great qualities and efforts, SrI ranganAtha himself listens to thiruvAimozhi kAlakshEpam from [mAmunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/23/azhagiya-manavala-mamunigal/) for a whole year and at the end of the lecture series, he himself appears as a small boy and accepts [mAmunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/23/azhagiya-manavala-mamunigal/) as his AchArya and submits him the most glorious “SrISailESa dhayApAthram…” thaniyan. Subsequently, many AchAryas who came in various lineages taught and lived by these dhivya prabandhams.

Thus, [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/)‘ dhivya prabandhams which were focussed on the upliftment of [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s which is to fulfill the desire of [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/) have been preserved by our [pUrvAchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/) through the history. It is emphasised greatly that all SrIvaishNavas should learn the dhivya prabandhams, understand the meanings properly and live by them. Keeping this in mind, with the aim of translating all of 4000 dhivya prabandhams, we are starting humbly in this great endeavour. We have a great desire to complete this translation kainkaryam by 2017 May, when we will commemorate the 1000th year appearance day of [SrI rAmAnuja](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/). Initially we are starting with English translation but we would try to arrange for translation of these dhivya prabandhams in other languages as well.

It would be very useful to understand the validity and glories of AzhwArs and their dhivya prabhandhams. Please read the following articles which go through them in detail:

With full dependence and faith on [SrIman nArAyaNan](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/), [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) and [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/), we are planning to translate the dhivya prabandhams into simple English (and other languages too). We will provide word-by-word meaning for each pAsuram with specific highlights and important points from the vyAkyAnams. The website will be well organized and easily searchable. We would also attempt to add pictures wherever possible/applicable. With the blessings of [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/), bhAgavathas and [AchAryas](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/introduction-contd/), we plan to do a faithful kainkaryam of translating these dhivya prabandhams which will be useful for many generations to come.

Though dhivya prabandham only indicates the thamizh pAsurams composed by [AzhwArs](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/azhwars/) (and rAmAnusa nURRanthAdhi of [thiruvarangaththu amudhanAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/03/26/thiruvarangathu-amudhanar/)), in this website/initiative, we would also cover many of the thamizh/samskritham prabandhams which are composed by our [pUrvAchAryas](http://guruparamparai.koyil.org) in the “[Other Prabandhams](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/index.php/others/)” section. We revere our AchAryas SrIsUkthis (divine compositions) greatly too and will also provide translation for the same as much as possible.

## sthOthra rathnam – 20
SrI: SrImathE SatakOpAya nama: SrImathE rAmAnujAya nama: SrImath varavaramunayE nama:

[Full Series](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/index.php/2016/12/sthothra-rathnam/)

[\<\< Previous](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/index.php/2017/01/sthothra-rathnam-19/)

[![Azhwars](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Azhwars.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Azhwars.jpg)

#### Introduction

ALavandhAr now explains the greatness of the devotees who are the enjoyers of the previously explained qualities of [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/). Alternative explanation – If there is so much greatness for those devotees who acquired such greatness from bhagavAn as said in brahma sUthram 1.1.20 “*asminnasya cha thadhyOgam SAsthi*” (Sruthi explains blissful togetherness of [jIvAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/) with [brahmam](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/) which is blissful), it is obvious that bhagavAn’s greatness cannot be measured.

*thvadhASrithAnAm jagadhudhbhavasthithi*  
*praNASasamsAravimOchanAdhaya: ।*  
*bhavanthi leelA vidhayaScha vaidhikAs*  
*thvadhIya gambhIra manOnusAriNa: ॥*

#### Word by word meaning (1)

thvadhASrithAnAm – for your devotees (like brahmA et al)  
jagath udhbhava sthithi praNASa samsAra vimOchana Adhaya: – creating the world, protecting, annihilating, helping to cross over the samsAra (material realm।), etc  
leelA bhavanthi – are like sports;  
vaidhikA: vidhaya: cha – the rules of vEdham too  
thvadhIya gambhIra manOnusAriNa: bhavanthi – follow the deep divine hearts of your devotees

#### Simple Translation (1)

For your devotees (like brahmA et al) creating the world, protecting, annihilating, helping to cross over the samsAra (material realm), etc., are like sports; the rules of vEdham too follow the deep divine hearts of your devotees.

#### Word by word meaning (2)

jagath udhbhava sthithi praNASa samsAra vimOchana Adhaya: – creating the world, protecting, annihilating, helping to cross over the samsAra, etc  
leelA – (your) sports  
thvath ASrithAnAm bhavanthi – exist for your devotees;  
vaidhikA: vidhaya: cha – the rules of vEdham too  
thvadhIya gambhIra manOnusAriNa: bhavanthi – follow your deep divine heart

#### Simple Translation (2)

Creating the world, protecting, annihilating, helping to cross over the samsAra, etc are your sports and they exist for your devotees only. The rules of vEdham too follows your deep divine heart

#### vyAkyAnam (Commentary)

- *thvadhASrithAnAm …* – brahmA, rudhra et al and the four mAnasa
  kumAras (sons) of brahmA viz sanaka et al and so on. If brahmA et al
  who acquired the skills of udhbhava (creation), sthithi (sustenance),
  praNASa (annihilation), samsAra vimOchanAdhaya: (lead others to
  liberation) etc., from you through your instructions and engage in in
  them, when those devotees who are exclusively existing for you are to
  do the same (creation etc), it will be for their sport. When the
  [mukthAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/06/thathva-thrayam-chith-who-am-i/)s
  (liberated souls) enjoy with you, they make their gyAthis (relatives)
  appear in front of them, through their sankalpam (divine will). brahma
  sUthram 4.4.8 “*sankalpAdhEva thachruthE:*” (mukthAthmAs create with
  their will. Since Sruthi (vEdham) says so). chAndhOgya upanishath
  8.2.1 “*sankalpAdhEvAsya pitharassamuththishtanthi*” (If the
  mukthAthmA desires to see his forefathers, those forefathers appear in
  front of him through his sankalpam (divine will)). They (liberated
  souls) do not engage in creation etc \[and only engage in serving
  [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/)
  in SrIvaikuNtam\], not because they are incapable but because they are
  not meant to do. “*Adhi*” (in the end of this part of the SlOkam –
  Adhaya:) indicates other aspects such as assuming multiple forms and
  controlling them etc. brahma sUthram 4.4.17 “*jagath vyApAra varjam*”
  (mukthAthmA’s wealth is \[exclusive engagement with brahmam only\]
  without any engagement in worldly creation etc; this is because
  creation etc., are attributed to brahmam only; also \[for mukthAthmA\]
  does not have the scope \[of such creation etc\]); brahma sUthram
  4.4.21 “*bhOga mAthra sAmya lingAchcha*” (due to mukthAthmA being
  identified as the one having equality \[with
  [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/)\]
  in enjoying brahmam, there is no engagement in creation etc \[for
  mukthAthmA\]).
- *jagadhudhbhavasthithi* *praNASasamsAravimOchanAdhaya: leelA
  thvadhASrithAnAm bhavanthi* – Alternative explanation – bhagavAn
  engages in creation etc based on the wishes of mumukshus (those
  devotees of bhagavAn who are desiring to be liberated). While
  bhagavAn’s nature is unsurpassed enjoyability, he is said as samsAra
  vimOchaka (one who liberates others from samsAram) due to the desires
  of his devotees. By “*Adhi*” (in the end of this part of the SlOkam –
  Adhaya:), his many incarnations for the aforementioned purpose, his
  assuming many forms during the divine rAsa krIdA (romantic sport) and
  so on.
- *vidhayaScha vaidhikA:* – Independent vaidhika rules such as “*idham
  kuru। idham mA kArshI: ॥*” (Do this; Don’t do this).
- *thvadhIya gambhIra manOnusAriNa:* – \[Such rules\] are following the
  deep hearts of those who are surrendered to you exclusively without
  any expectation. *gambhIrathva (*depth) for manas (mind/heart) is –
  instead of engaging in lowly wealth (worldly wealth) etc and being
  *ananya prayOjana* (exclusively pleasing
  [bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/)
  only). SvEthASvathara upanishath 1.1 “*brahmavAdhinO vadhanthi*”
  (brahmavAdhis (knowers of brahmam/bhagavAn) are saying); thaiththirIya
  AraNyaka 1.9 “*sa hOvAcha vyAsa: pArASarya:*” (Spoken by sage
  parASara’s son, illustrious vyAsa); thaiththirIya upanishath
  SikshAvalli 11 “*atha yathi thE karma vichikithsA vA vruththa
  vichikithsA vA syAth । yE thathra brAhmaNAs sammarSina: । yukthA
  AyukthA: । alUkshA dharmakAmA: syu: । yathA thE thathra varthEran ।
  thathA thathra varthEthA: ।*” (If you get any doubts in karma (ritual
  activities) or AchAram (practices), you follow what is done by
  brAhmaNas who are experts in analysis, engaged in vEdha SAsthram,
  capable in all matters, devoid of kAma (lust) and krOdha (anger) and
  desirous of dharma (virtuous activities)). For those who are not fully
  engaged in bhagavath vishayam (matters related to bhagavAn) as defined
  in thiruvAimozhi 9.6.2 “*ninaithoRum sollum thoRum nenjidindhugum*”
  (every time I think about him, every time I speak/hear about him, my
  heart becomes broken), SAsthram ordains \[to engage them in him in a
  procedural manner\] as in bruhadhAraNyaka upanishath 6.6.6
  “*nidhidhyASithavya:*”
  (\[[bhagavAn](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/)\]
  should be meditated upon), bruhadhAraNyaka upanishath 6.4.21
  “*vigyAya pragyAm kurvItha brAhmaNa:*” (Knowledgeable brAhmaNa should
  know about that
  [paramAthmA](https://granthams.koyil.org/2013/03/12/thathva-thrayam-iswara-who-is-god/)
  through hearing and thinking and meditate upon him) and muNdaka
  upanishath 2.2.6 “*OmithyEva dhyAyathAthmAnam*” (Meditate upon
  paramAthmA, considering him as “Om”). Alternative explanation – Also
  explained as following your deep heart. vishNu dharmam 6.31 “*Sruthi:
  smruthir mamaivAgyA yasthAmullanghya varththathE । AgyAchchEthI mam
  dhrOhI madhbhakthO’pi na vaishNava: ॥*” (vEdham and smruthi are my
  orders. One who fails to follow such orders, is considered to have
  violated my orders; he is a traitor; even if he is my devotee, he is
  not a vaishNava).

आळवंदार्, काट्टुमन्नार् कोयिल्

ई ग्रंथमुनकु उन्न तनियन्लनु तॆलुसुकुंदां.

*स्वादयन्निह सर्वेषां त्रय्यंतार्थं सुदुर्ग्रहं ।*  
*स्तोत्रयामास योगींद्रः तं वन्दे यामुनाह्वयं॥*

ग्राह्यमुनकु दुर्गमैन वेदांत रहस्यमुलनु सुलभमुगा ग्राह्यमगुनट्लु तम स्तोत्ररत्नमुन विशदीकरिंचिन, योगुललो श्रेष्ठुलैन यामुनाचार्युलकु वंदनं.

*यत् पदां भोरुह ध्यान विध्वस्ताशेषकल्मषः ।*  
*वस्तुतामुपयातो2हं यामुनेयं नमामि तं॥*

ऎवरि दिव्यकृपतो ना कल्मषमुलन्नी नाशनमु चेंदिनवो मरियु ऒक वस्तुवुगा गुर्तिंचबड्डानो पूर्वमु असत् (अचेतनमु)गा उंडि यामुनाचार्युल पादमुल ध्यानमुतो प्रस्तुतं सत्(आत्म/ चेतनमु) गा भाविस्तुन्नानो आश्री यामुनाचार्युलनकु नमस्करिस्तुन्नानु.
