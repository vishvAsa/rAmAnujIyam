## प्राग्-आचारः
Panca kala kriya deepa recommends that we should do somayaga while meditating on Narayana. 

Keshava Somayaji, Somasi Andaan, Tatacharyas etc all did somayaga.  
First 22 yatis of parakala matha were all agnihotris and thus were cremated (no brindavanam for them).

satakratu TT did gosava. It is recorded.

## न कलिवर्ज्यम्
It is not kali-varjya. 

## पशुः
> surapuram TTs. Some did sapashu while some did pishtapashu. It was mostly based on the personal leaning of the yajamana. That's all.

Sri Vaishnava Samayachara Nishkarsha by piLLai-lokaM jIyar recomments piShTa-pashus.
