## भावना
उक्तस्थानेषु नाडिशोधनं तिलकधरणेन कल्पयेत्।  
श्वेत-मृत्तिका श्वेतद्वीपमृत्तिका-प्रतिनिधिर् इति स्मरेत्।  
पादाकृतिं धार्यमाणम् भावयेत्। 

पुण्ड्रेषु केशवादीन् आवाहयेत् ।  

### Shape matters
Some say that tilaka shape doesn't matter. If shape doesn't matter, might as well put a tiryak puNDra; and deshika would have wasted time classifying shapes per adhikAri.

- नाभौ, (नाभ्यादि १२-अङ्गुलम् ऊर्ध्वं, ३-अङ्गुलं विस्तृतं)

### Evidence
One can infer much (with high confidence) from carved tilaka-s. Assuming mutiliation in the absence of specific evidence would violate parsimony.

Easy to convert TK to VK with a file, but not vice versa. That would be much harder (would need to trim vertical lines fully, besides carving the horizontal bit), and the modification would be much easier to detect.

> AP and telengana - vaikhanasas generally sport so called vdk namam. The vaikhanasa pathasalas also sport that style of namam only.
