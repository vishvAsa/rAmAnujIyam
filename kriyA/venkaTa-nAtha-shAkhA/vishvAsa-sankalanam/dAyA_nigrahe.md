## भ्रमः
> When bhagavAn can have dayA on all creatures, who are we to take enjoyment from of criticizing others?  
> … sarva-bhUta-dayA-puShpam

## दयायां विवेकः
"sarva-bhUta-dayA" etc. is not a blanket mandate,  
and it has clear exceptions in case of bhagavad-bhAgavata-pratikUla-s.  

There, dayA can be said to take the form of nigraha.

## भगवत्-प्रतिकूल-निग्रह-भोग्यता
भगवद्-भुक्त-शेष-भुजो हि भागवताः।  
भगवद्-अभीष्टे खल-निग्रह-लीला-रसे, तच्छेषं सानन्दं भुञ्ज्महय् इति।  

द्वार-पालादिषु भगवद्-भटेषु स-शस्त्रेषु सत्सु  
खल-निग्रह-भोग्यतैव शिक्षणीया।  
(वग्-वज्रास् तु ब्राह्मणाः। ) 
भक्ताङ्घ्रि-रेणूक्तयस् तु सन्ति।

If dayA were indiscriminate, one should explain where the "dayA" (as normally understood) is towards the buddhus mentioned by the ALHvAr here. Why are such verses memorized to date, and taught to little kids?

Of course, today there are more deadly threats today compared to the shramaNas and bauddha-s,  
and major sections of these have given up betrayal,  
have sought our refuge  
and are allied with us against the unmAda-s. 

But mutatis mutandis, the attitude of punishing the wicked with violence remains.
