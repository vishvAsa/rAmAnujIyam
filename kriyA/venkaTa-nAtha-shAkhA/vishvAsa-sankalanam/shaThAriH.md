## माध्व-भ्रमः

### गोश्रीपुर-मन्दिरे रविलोचनः
"shaThakop" is not offered to devotees (They don't want to touch the devotees due to maDi). Offered only to parivara devatas (Hanuman, garuda, ganapati, navagrahas and siva).
